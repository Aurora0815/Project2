World Rice Export

From this choropleth map, we can see that in addition to India and Thailand, the major rice exporters are the United States, Pakistan, etc.

This stacked bar chart shows the types of rice exported by the world's major rice exporters. The most traded type of rice is milled rice.

The area chart shows the evolution of the rice export market concentration. We can see that the vast majority of rice exporters are concentrated in Asia.

From 2006 to 2011, the country that exported the most rice in the world was Thailand. At the end of 2011, India surpassed Thailand to become the largest exporter of rice, and since 2012, India is still the largest exporter of rice.

The pie chart shows the export ratio of Thai rice, and the most is still milled rice.
